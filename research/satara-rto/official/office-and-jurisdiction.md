## Official portals used by citizens

- Maharashtra Transport Department
  - State service pages, office list, fee tables, tax tables, faceless-service status.
- Sarathi / Parivahan
  - Driving licence services, appointment flows, fee payments, learner and driving licence workflows.
- Vahan / Parivahan
  - Vehicle-related services such as transfer, NOC, RC renewal, address change, hypothecation, tax, and fitness.

## Routing guidance for a website

Because Satara district has both `MH-11` and `MH-50`, a Satara-focused website should not assume a single office.

Recommended routing inputs:

- Taluka
- Pincode
- Vehicle registration code if already available
- Whether the user's existing record is under MH-11 or MH-50
- Whether the service is licence-side or vehicle-side

## Practical office-visit model

### Mostly online or faceless

- Learner's licence
- DL renewal
- DL duplicate
- DL address change
- Some RC-related services under Vahan faceless flows

### Online application plus office visit

- Permanent driving licence
- International Driving Permit
- New vehicle registration
- Fitness certificate / renewal of fitness
- Cases requiring vehicle inspection or original document verification

### Online application plus possible office visit or hearing

- Permit services
- Commercial vehicle clearances
- Complex ownership or financier cases

## Product implication

A Satara RTO website should ask the user early:

- Which office is linked to your record?
- Is this a licence service or vehicle service?
- Does your case involve a vehicle inspection, permit, or finance company?

That split removes a large amount of confusion before the user reaches forms or payments.
