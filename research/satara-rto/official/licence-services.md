# Official Licence Services

This file covers the main licence services relevant to Satara users, based on Maharashtra Transport Department and Parivahan sources.

## 1. Learner's licence

### Eligibility

- Regular motor vehicle categories: generally `18+`
- Gearless motorcycle up to 50cc: generally `16+` with guardian consent
- Transport vehicle: generally `20+`

### Official process

- Go to `sarathi.parivahan.gov.in`
- Select Maharashtra
- Start the learner's licence flow
- Fill application details
- Upload required proofs
- Pay the learner's licence fee and test fee
- Complete the learner's test as instructed by the portal / state flow
- Receive learner's licence after approval

### Office visit

- Maharashtra also runs faceless learner's licence processing for supported cases
- A website should still warn users that the portal may require state-specific verification steps

### Required documents

- Age proof
- Address proof
- Form 2 application details
- Form 1 or Form 1A, depending on vehicle class / medical requirement
- Photos if requested by the selected workflow

### Fees

- Issue of learner's licence: `Rs. 150` per class of vehicle
- Learner's licence test fee: `Rs. 50`

### Validity

- Learner's licence validity: `6 months`

## 2. Permanent driving licence

### Eligibility

- User must hold a valid learner's licence
- Maharashtra states the user can apply for the driving test after `30 days` from learner's licence issue

### Official process

- Apply through Sarathi / Parivahan for permanent licence
- Book an appointment for the driving test
- Visit the RTO on the appointment date
- Appear for the driving skill test
- After approval, the licence is issued

### Office visit

- Yes
- Driving test at the RTO is the core step

### Required documents

- `Form 4`
- Learner's licence
- Recent passport photos
- Age proof
- Address proof
- `Form 5` for transport category applications

### Fees

- Driving competence test fee: `Rs. 300` per class
- Issue of driving licence: `Rs. 200`

### Delivery timeline

- Official sources do not publish a Satara-specific fixed SLA
- Delivery depends on test pass, approval, and card / dispatch processing

## 3. Driving licence renewal

### Eligibility and timing

- Maharashtra says the user may apply from `30 days before expiry`
- If applied within the grace window, renewal is effective from the original expiry date
- Maharashtra states licence renewal is not allowed after `5 years` from expiry
- Parivahan notes that cases beyond `5 years` need fresh licence formalities

### Official process

- Open `sarathi.parivahan.gov.in`
- Select Maharashtra
- Choose services on driving licence
- Select renewal
- Fill details and upload documents
- Pay online
- Complete portal verification
- Visit the RTO if the portal or state process asks for original verification

### Office visit

- Possible
- Maharashtra lists renewal as faceless-enabled, but a website should still warn users that some records may be flagged for verification

### Required documents

- `Form 9`
- Current driving licence
- `Form 1A` medical certificate
- Recent photographs
- If licence is from another state: NOC or verification document from the original licensing authority

### Fees

- Renewal of driving licence: `Rs. 200`
- Renewal after grace period: `Rs. 300`
- Additional late fee after the grace period: `Rs. 1000` per year or part thereof, as per the central fee table

### Delivery timeline

- No fixed Satara-specific timeline published

## 4. Duplicate driving licence

### When available

- Licence lost
- Licence damaged / torn / defaced
- Photo replacement need

### Official process

- Use Sarathi driving licence services
- Select duplicate licence
- Fill details
- Upload documents
- Pay online
- Complete verification
- Visit the RTO only if required by the selected flow

### Office visit

- Usually not the first step in faceless flow
- May still be required in exception cases

### Required documents

- `LLD` / duplicate licence form as listed by Maharashtra
- Old damaged licence if available
- Police report if licence is lost
- Recent passport photos
- Attested photocopy of DL, if available, helps in lost cases

### Fees

- Maharashtra duplicate licence page shows `Rs. 214`
- Portal totals may differ if extra user charges apply

## 5. Change of address in driving licence

### Official process

- Open `sarathi.parivahan.gov.in`
- Select Maharashtra
- Choose services on driving licence
- Enter DL number and date of birth
- Select change of address
- Upload address proof
- Pay fee
- Complete portal verification

### Office visit

- Maharashtra lists this as faceless-enabled
- A website should still show a fallback note for record-mismatch cases

### Required documents

- Existing driving licence
- New address proof
- Application details under the Sarathi service flow
- Any supporting identity document requested by the portal

### Fees

- Change in address / particulars in driving licence: `Rs. 200`

## 6. International Driving Permit

### Eligibility

- Applicant must hold a valid Indian driving licence
- Applicant must be a resident of India

### Official process

- Apply under the IDP flow through Sarathi / Parivahan or as instructed on the state page
- Submit IDP application details
- Pay the fee
- Visit the licensing authority / RTO within jurisdiction
- Present travel and identity documents for verification

### Office visit

- Yes
- IDP requires verification before issuance

### Required documents

- `Form 4A`
- Valid Indian driving licence
- Passport
- Visa, where applicable
- Air ticket or travel proof for verification
- `Form 1A`
- Photos, as listed by Maharashtra

### Fees

- Central fee table: `Rs. 1000`
- Maharashtra fee page also shows `Rs. 1000`

### Validity

- Typically issued for `1 year`

## Notes for website design

- The site should clearly separate `faceless`, `appointment required`, and `RTO visit required`.
- Renewal and duplicate flows should show exception handling for missing old records, out-of-state licences, and expired licences.
- A document checklist should be class-aware: non-transport vs transport.
