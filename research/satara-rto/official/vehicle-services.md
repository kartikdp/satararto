# Official Vehicle Services

This file covers the main vehicle document services relevant to Satara users.

## 1. New vehicle registration

### Official process

- Dealer-point registration and online services are available in Maharashtra for many cases
- The vehicle must be presented for inspection where required
- Registration is completed after document verification, tax payment, fee payment, and assignment of registration number

### Office visit

- Usually yes, directly or via dealer-assisted registration flow
- Maharashtra notes physical inspection by the inspecting authority

### Required documents

- `Form 20`
- `Form 21` sale certificate
- `Form 22` or `Form 22A` road-worthiness / compliance certificate
- Purchase invoice
- Temporary registration, if applicable
- PAN card or `Form 60` / `Form 61`, generally except two-wheelers where applicable
- Valid insurance certificate
- Address proof
- `Form 34` if financed
- Fitness certificate if applicable
- Road tax payment

### Fees

- Registration fee depends on vehicle class
- Examples from the central fee table:
  - Motorcycle: `Rs. 300`
  - LMV non-transport: `Rs. 600`
  - LMV transport: `Rs. 1000`
- Smart-card RC may add `Rs. 200`
- Motor vehicle tax is separate and category-based

## 2. Transfer of ownership

### Timing

- Maharashtra states:
  - Same jurisdiction: `14 days`
  - Other registering authority jurisdiction: `30 days`
- Parivahan also distinguishes same-state and outside-state cases

### Official process

- Apply online through Vahan where available
- Enter registration and owner details
- Upload transfer documents
- Pay transfer fee and tax dues if any
- Attend verification if the case requires it

### Office visit

- Possible
- More likely in document-mismatch, financed, or cross-jurisdiction cases

### Required documents

- `Form 29`
- `Form 30`
- `Form 28` if purchaser belongs to another registering authority jurisdiction
- RC
- Insurance certificate
- PUC certificate
- PAN card of seller and purchaser or `Form 60`
- Address proof
- Chassis / engine pencil print if asked
- Tax clearance, where applicable
- Purchaser undertaking and photos where required by the case

### Fees

- Transfer of ownership fee is `half` of the registration fee under Rule 81
- Example implications:
  - Motorcycle transfer: `Rs. 150`
  - LMV non-transport transfer: `Rs. 300`
  - LMV transport transfer: `Rs. 500`

## 3. No Objection Certificate (NOC)

### When needed

- Vehicle is being removed or sold outside the current registering authority jurisdiction
- Often used before out-of-state transfer or re-registration

### Official process

- Apply under Vahan / Parivahan using the NOC service
- Submit vehicle details
- Upload documents
- Pay fee
- Complete clearance checks

### Office visit

- Possible, especially for transport vehicles and clearance-heavy cases

### Required documents

- `Form 28`
- Certified copy of RC
- Certified copy of insurance
- Up-to-date motor vehicle tax proof
- PUC certificate
- Chassis / engine pencil print where required
- Signature identification of owner
- For transport vehicles:
  - Permit surrender or permit status evidence
  - Challan clearance
  - Tax clearance
  - Fitness clearance

### Fees

- Fee is payable under Rule 81
- Exact amount should be confirmed on the live portal

## 4. RC renewal

### Timing

- Apply not more than `60 days` before RC expiry

### Official process

- Use Vahan renewal of RC flow
- Login / verify via OTP
- Fill service details
- Upload documents
- Book appointment if prompted
- Pay fees
- Visit RTO with originals where required

### Office visit

- Often yes, especially if inspection or verification is required

### Required documents

- `Form 25`
- PUC certificate
- RC / RC book
- Fitness certificate where applicable
- Road tax payment proof
- Insurance certificate
- PAN card or `Form 60` / `Form 61`
- Chassis / engine pencil print
- Signature identification of owner

### Fees

- Renewal uses the registration-fee table by vehicle class
- Smart-card fee may apply
- Delay fee:
  - Motorcycle: `Rs. 300` per month or part thereof
  - Other non-transport vehicles: `Rs. 500` per month or part thereof

## 5. Duplicate RC

### When needed

- RC lost
- RC destroyed
- RC torn, mutilated, or used up

### Official process

- Report loss to police
- Intimate the registering authority
- Apply in Vahan / last registering authority flow
- Upload proof and pay fee

### Office visit

- Possible, especially if originals / affidavit / commercial clearances are involved

### Required documents

- `Form 26`
- Police certificate or FIR
- PUC certificate
- Insurance certificate
- Address proof
- PAN card or `Form 60` / `Form 61`
- Chassis / engine pencil print
- Signature identification
- Affidavit that RC is lost and not impounded
- Commercial vehicle challan and tax clearances where applicable

### Fees

- Duplicate RC fee is `half` of the registration fee

## 6. Change of address in RC

### Timing

- Apply within `14 days` of address change

### Official process

- Use Vahan address-change service
- Enter registration and chassis details
- Verify via OTP
- Fill service details
- Upload documents
- Pay fee

### Office visit

- Possible if original verification or financier approval is needed

### Required documents

- `Form 33`
- RC
- New address proof
- Insurance certificate
- PUC certificate
- Financier NOC if hypothecated
- PAN card or `Form 60` / `Form 61`
- Chassis / engine pencil print
- Signature identification

### Fees

- Change of residence fee is `half` of registration fee
- Smart-card fee may apply

## 7. Hypothecation addition

### Official process

- Use Vahan hypothecation endorsement flow
- Submit vehicle and financier details
- Upload documents
- Pay fee

### Office visit

- Possible if signatures or originals need in-person verification

### Required documents

- `Form 34`
- RC
- Financier and owner signatures
- Insurance certificate
- PUC certificate
- Address proof if requested
- PAN card or `Form 60` / `Form 61`
- Chassis / engine pencil print

### Fees

- Endorsing hire-purchase / lease / hypothecation:
  - Motorcycle: `Rs. 500`
  - Three-wheeler / quadricycle / LMV: `Rs. 1500`
  - Medium / heavy vehicle: `Rs. 3000`

## 8. Hypothecation removal

### Official process

- Use Vahan HP termination service
- Submit loan-closure details
- Upload financier clearance
- Pay if applicable
- Get fresh RC / updated record after approval

### Office visit

- Possible
- Financier-related cases often need careful verification

### Required documents

- `Form 35`
- RC
- Financier due-clearance / NOC
- Insurance certificate
- PUC certificate
- PAN card or `Form 60` / `Form 61`
- Chassis / engine pencil print
- Signature identification

### Fees

- Parivahan fee table clarifies no separate fee for cancellation of lease / hypothecation and issue of fresh RC thereafter

## 9. Fitness certificate

### Applicable mainly to

- Transport vehicles

### Official process

- Use Vahan fitness service
- Login on Vahan
- Enter registration number and chassis number
- Verify via OTP
- Fill fitness details
- Fill insurance details
- Upload documents in DMS
- Book appointment
- Pay online
- Visit RTO with originals and vehicle for inspection

### Office visit

- Yes
- Vehicle inspection is central to this service

### Required documents

- Vehicle registration details
- Insurance details
- Existing fitness details where applicable
- Uploads requested by the portal
- Original documents at the office visit

### Fees

- Conducting test for grant / renewal of fitness:
  - Motorcycle manual: `Rs. 200`
  - Three-wheeler / LMV manual: `Rs. 400`
  - Medium / heavy manual: `Rs. 600`
- Grant or renewal of certificate of fitness: `Rs. 200`
- Delay after fitness expiry: `Rs. 50` per day

## 10. Reassignment / vehicle brought from another state

This is not a basic Satara-only service, but it matters for NOC-driven journeys.

### Trigger

- Vehicle from another state kept in Maharashtra for more than `12 months`

### Required documents

- `Form 27`
- RC
- Proof of residence
- NOC
- Insurance certificate
- PUC certificate
- Form 28 / Form 20 where required
- Challan clearance for commercial vehicles
- Fitness certificate where applicable
- PAN / Form 60 / Form 61
- Chassis / engine pencil print

### Product note

Users often do not realize NOC and reassignment are linked steps. A website should connect them as one journey.
