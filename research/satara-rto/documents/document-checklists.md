# Document Checklists

This file groups the research into reusable document bundles so a website can generate service-specific checklists without repeating the same logic everywhere.

## 1. Core personal proofs

These are reused across many licence and vehicle services.

- Age proof
- Address proof
- PAN card
- `Form 60` / `Form 61` where PAN is not applicable
- Passport-size photographs
- Signature identification

## 2. Core driving licence bundle

- Existing DL
- Learner's licence
- `Form 1` or self-declaration where applicable
- `Form 1A` medical certificate
- `Form 2`
- `Form 4`
- `Form 4A`
- `Form 5` for transport class applications
- Police report in lost-licence cases
- Old defaced licence if available

## 3. Core vehicle bundle

- RC / RC book
- Insurance certificate
- PUC certificate
- Tax receipt / tax clearance
- Chassis and engine pencil print
- Address proof of owner

## 4. New vehicle registration bundle

- `Form 20`
- `Form 21`
- `Form 22` / `Form 22A`
- Purchase invoice
- Temporary registration
- Insurance certificate
- Address proof
- PAN or `Form 60` / `Form 61`
- `Form 34` if vehicle is financed
- Fitness certificate if applicable
- Tax payment proof

## 5. Ownership transfer bundle

- `Form 29`
- `Form 30`
- `Form 28` where another registering authority jurisdiction is involved
- RC
- Insurance certificate
- PUC certificate
- Tax clearance or proof of tax paid
- PAN / `Form 60`
- Address proof of purchaser
- Chassis and engine pencil print
- Purchaser undertaking where required
- Passport photo where required

## 6. NOC bundle

- `Form 28`
- RC copy
- Insurance copy
- Tax proof up to date
- PUC certificate
- Chassis and engine pencil print
- Owner signature identification

### Additional transport-vehicle NOC clearances

- Permit surrender / permit status evidence
- Challan clearance
- Tax clearance
- Fitness clearance

## 7. RC renewal bundle

- `Form 25`
- RC
- PUC certificate
- Insurance certificate
- Tax payment proof
- Fitness certificate where applicable
- PAN / `Form 60` / `Form 61`
- Chassis and engine pencil print
- Signature identification

## 11. Hypothecation removal bundle

## 12. Fitness certificate bundle

- Vehicle registration details
- Insurance details
- Existing fitness record where applicable
- Portal-uploaded documents requested by Vahan

- Vehicle presented for inspection

## 13. International Driving Permit bundle

- `Form 4A`
- Valid Indian driving licence
- Passport
- Visa where applicable
- Air ticket / travel proof
- `Form 1A`
- Photos

## 14. Website checklist logic recommendations

The checklist generator should ask:

- Which service do you need?
- Is this non-transport or transport?
- Is the vehicle financed?
- Is the document lost / damaged / expired?
- Is the vehicle moving to another state?
- Are you applying under MH-11 or MH-50?

That logic will remove most of the checklist confusion users face on portals.
