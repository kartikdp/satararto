# Forms Reference

This is a compact reference for the forms repeatedly mentioned in the official sources.

## Driving licence forms

- `Form 1`
  - Self declaration of physical fitness in applicable cases
- `Form 1A`
  - Medical certificate
- `Form 2`
  - Application form used in learner / DL related workflows on Parivahan
- `Form 3`
  - Learner's licence
- `Form 4`
  - Application for permanent driving licence
- `Form 4A`
  - Application for International Driving Permit
- `Form 5`
  - Driving certificate from driving school / establishment, commonly used for transport category applications
- `LLD`
  - Duplicate driving licence application form referenced on Maharashtra's duplicate licence page

## Vehicle registration forms

- `Form 20`
  - Application for registration of a motor vehicle
- `Form 20B`
  - Extension of temporary registration
- `Form 21`
  - Sale certificate
- `Form 22`
  - Certificate of road-worthiness
- `Form 22A`
  - Compliance / road-worthiness certificate for separately fabricated bodies and related cases
- `Form 23A`
  - RC smart card / electronic medium format
- `Form 23B`
  - Temporary certificate of registration
- `Form 24`
  - Registration of motor vehicle
- `Form 25`
  - Renewal of certificate of registration
- `Form 26`
  - Duplicate RC
- `Form 27`
  - Assignment of new registration mark / reassignment
- `Form 28`
  - No Objection Certificate
- `Form 29`
  - Notice of transfer of ownership
- `Form 30`
  - Application for intimation and transfer of ownership
- `Form 31`
  - Transfer of ownership in case of death of owner / successor case
- `Form 32`
  - Transfer of ownership in case of public auction
- `Form 33`
  - Change of address in RC
- `Form 34`
  - Entry of hire-purchase / lease / hypothecation after registration
- `Form 35`
  - Termination of hire-purchase / lease / hypothecation
- `Form 36`
  - Fresh RC in the name of financier in default-driven cases

## Fitness forms

- `Form 38`
  - Certificate of fitness for transport vehicle cases
- `Form 38A`
  - Report of inspection

## Permit forms

- `Form 45`
  - Tourist vehicle permit application
- `Form 46`
  - Tourist permit / national permit authorization application
- `Form 47`
  - Authorization for tourist permit / national permit
- `Form 48`
  - National permit application

## Product note

The website should not only show form numbers. It should translate each form into plain-language purpose, who signs it, and when it becomes necessary.
